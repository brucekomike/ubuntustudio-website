## Information Channels

These are all the channels and methods used for communication within the Ubuntu Studio development community.

### IRC at irc.freenode.net

- #ubuntustudio-devel
- #ubuntustudio (user channel)

IRC chatting is the quickest and most common communication method used in Ubuntu Studio development.

* [IRC HowTo](http://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuStudio/IRC) - *for those not familiar with IRC*

### Mail lists

- [ubuntu-studio-users mail list](http://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/Ubuntu-Studio-users) - user mail list
- [ubuntu-studio-devel mail list](http://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/Ubuntu-Studio-devel) - general development discussion
- [ubuntustudio-bugs mail list](http://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/Ubuntustudio-bugs) - not active atm
- [ubuntustudio-testing list](http:/lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntustudio-testing) - A place where to post testing results
