## [Wallpaper](Wallpaper)

Open to any suggestion.

- Thumbnails should be no greater in height than 300px. Link to larger images.
- Must be a .png or .svg @2560x1600.
- If it's a .svg it need only be a 16:10 aspect ratio.
- Tasteful branding is OK.
