# Introduction

Good communication with other wiki contributors is essential to writing great resources. Some guidelines:

# General Communication

- [IRC](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InternetRelayChat) - #ubuntu-doc on irc.libera.chat
- Mailing lists - [ubuntu-doc](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-doc)
- Comments - When saving your edits, always leave a comment so others can easily see what you did.

# Page Categories and Linking

- Review - [Tag your pages](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Tag) when they have not been reviewed, and discuss your contributions on the [docteam mailing list](http://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-doc). If you let us know about it, it can be reviewed more quickly and its tags removed.
- Do not add a link to an unreviewed page on the wiki's index pages (eg. [UserDocumentation](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UserDocumentation) or [Multimedia](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Multimedia)) until the page has been reviewed and is not tagged.

# Subscribing to Pages

- Subscribe to the pages you have contributed to. To do this, click on the Subscribe link of the page you wish to subscribe to.
